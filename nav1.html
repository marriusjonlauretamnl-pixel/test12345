Leon S. Kennedy

Posted on April 7, 2026

Introduction

Leon S. Kennedy is one of the most iconic characters in the Resident Evil series. He first appeared in Resident Evil 2 (1998) as a rookie police officer on his first day in Raccoon City, immediately thrown into the chaos of a deadly viral outbreak. Despite being inexperienced, Leon quickly demonstrates courage, quick thinking, and resourcefulness as he navigates life-threatening situations filled with zombies and other bio-organic threats.

Over the years, Leon has grown from a rookie cop into a highly skilled government agent specializing in counter-bioterrorism operations. He is known for his calm and professional demeanor under extreme pressure, exceptional combat skills, and unwavering dedication to protecting civilians and completing dangerous missions. Leon often works alone but is also capable of forming effective partnerships with other characters, such as Ada Wong and Claire Redfield.

His experiences in Raccoon City, Europe, and around the world have shaped him into a strategic and reliable operative, able to face deadly viral outbreaks, rogue organizations, and highly dangerous bio-weapons. Leon’s intelligence, courage, and moral integrity have made him a fan-favorite character and a symbol of resilience, professionalism, and heroism within the Resident Evil franchise.

Games Leon has appeared on


  • Resident Evil 2 (1998) – Leon’s first mission as a rookie police officer in Raccoon City.
  • Resident Evil 4 (2005) – Leon is a government agent sent to rescue the U.S. President’s daughter.
  • Resident Evil 6 (2012) – One of the main protagonists facing a global bio-terrorist threat.
  • Resident Evil: Operation Raccoon City (2012) – Leon appears as a supporting character.
  • Resident Evil: Revelations 2 (2015) – Leon makes a cameo in some story segments.
  • Resident Evil 2 Remake (2019) – Modernized retelling of his first mission in Raccoon City.
  • Resident Evil 4 Remake (2023) – Remake of his classic mission to save Ashley Graham.

Ada Wong

Posted on April 7, 2026

Introduction

Ada Wong is one of the most mysterious and enigmatic characters in the Resident Evil series. Known for her intelligence, resourcefulness, and exceptional combat skills, she often operates independently as a spy with secret objectives that are rarely revealed. Her true motives remain unclear, which adds to her intrigue and unpredictability within the series.

She first appeared in Resident Evil 2 (1998), where she became involved in the deadly viral outbreak in Raccoon City. Since then, Ada has crossed paths multiple times with Leon S. Kennedy, forming a complex relationship that blends rivalry, trust, and occasional collaboration.

Ada is also known for her agility, clever use of gadgets, and ability to adapt quickly to dangerous situations. Her presence in the series often influences the outcome of major events, whether she is working for her own agenda or secretly assisting key protagonists. Over the years, Ada Wong has become one of the most iconic and enduring characters in the Resident Evil franchise.

Trivia

  • First appeared in Resident Evil 2 (1998) as a spy with secret objectives during the Raccoon City outbreak.
  • Often works independently, but occasionally assists Leon S. Kennedy while following her own agenda.
  • Highly skilled in combat, espionage, infiltration, and using various gadgets.
  • Known for her calm, mysterious, and unpredictable personality, which makes her interactions intriguing.
  • Frequently crosses paths with major characters, influencing key events in the Resident Evil universe.
  • Her signature look includes her red dress and concealed weapons, which became iconic in the series.

Games Ada has appeared on


  • Resident Evil 2 (1998) – Ada appears as a spy investigating Umbrella Corporation during the Raccoon City outbreak.
  • Resident Evil 4 (2005) – Assists Leon covertly while pursuing her own secret mission.
  • Resident Evil: The Umbrella Chronicles (2007) – Appears in key story events that retell previous Resident Evil incidents.
  • Resident Evil 6 (2012) – Plays a supporting role, helping Leon while following her espionage agenda.
  • Resident Evil: Operation Raccoon City (2012) – Appears as a playable character in specific missions.
  • Resident Evil 2 Remake (2019) – Modernized retelling of her original 1998 role in Raccoon City.
  • Resident Evil 4 Remake (2023) – Returns in her espionage role, aiding Leon while pursuing secret objectives.
  • Crossovers and spin-offs – Ada has appeared in games like Marvel vs. Capcom 3 and Project X Zone.

Chris Redfield

Posted on April 7, 2026

Introduction

Chris Redfield is a highly skilled and determined character in the Resident Evil series. He first appeared in Resident Evil (1996) as a member of the elite Special Tactics and Rescue Service (S.T.A.R.S.), tasked with investigating strange incidents in the Arklay Mountains. Chris quickly became known for his bravery, combat expertise, and unwavering commitment to stopping bio-terrorism.

Over the years, Chris has grown into a veteran bio-terrorism operative, taking on dangerous missions around the world. He is recognized for his exceptional strength, tactical intelligence, and resilience in the face of overwhelming threats. Chris often works alongside other key characters, such as Jill Valentine, Leon S. Kennedy, and Claire Redfield, forming strong partnerships in high-stakes operations.

Known for his moral integrity and dedication to protecting civilians, Chris has confronted countless viral outbreaks and rogue organizations. His physical prowess, investigative skills, and leadership abilities have made him one of the most respected and enduring protagonists in the Resident Evil franchise.

Trivia

  • First appeared in Resident Evil (1996) as a member of S.T.A.R.S., investigating incidents in the Arklay Mountains.
  • Veteran bio-terrorism operative known for exceptional strength and combat skills.
  • Brother of Claire Redfield; often collaborates with her in missions against viral threats.
  • Highly skilled in firearms, hand-to-hand combat, and tactical operations.
  • Strong moral compass and dedication to protecting civilians and stopping bio-terrorism.
  • Appears in numerous games, movies, and other media, cementing his status as a fan-favorite character.

Games Chris has appeared on


  • Resident Evil (1996) – First appearance as a S.T.A.R.S. member in the Arklay Mountains incident.
  • Resident Evil Code: Veronica (2000) – Main protagonist working to rescue his sister Claire and fight Umbrella.
  • Resident Evil 5 (2009) – Leads mission in Africa against a new viral outbreak.
  • Resident Evil 6 (2012) – Main protagonist dealing with global bio-terror threats.
  • Resident Evil: Revelations 2 (2015) – Plays a supporting role in key missions.
  • Resident Evil 7: Biohazard (VR cameo) – Minor appearance.
  • Crossovers and spin-offs – Appears in games like Marvel vs. Capcom and Project X Zone.

Claire Redfield

Posted on April 7, 2026

Introduction

Claire Redfield is a courageous, determined, and resourceful character in the Resident Evil series. She first appeared in Resident Evil 2 (1998) as a college student searching for her brother, Chris Redfield, amidst the deadly viral outbreak in Raccoon City. Despite being untrained at the time, Claire quickly adapts to dangerous situations, showing remarkable intelligence, resilience, and quick thinking in life-threatening moments.

Over the years, Claire has evolved from a concerned sister into a skilled survivor and fighter against bio-terrorism. She is known for her strong moral compass, bravery, and determination to protect innocent people caught in the chaos caused by the Umbrella Corporation and other bio-organic threats. Her sense of justice often drives her to take risks in order to help others, even when it puts her own life in danger.

Claire’s experiences throughout the series have strengthened her tactical skills, combat abilities, and leadership qualities, making her a respected and iconic figure among Resident Evil characters. She has become a symbol of hope and resilience, often guiding and protecting those who are vulnerable during outbreaks. Her dedication, courage, and unwavering spirit have cemented her place as one of the most beloved protagonists in the franchise.

Trivia

  • First appeared in Resident Evil 2 (1998), searching for her brother Chris during the Raccoon City outbreak.
  • Chris Redfield’s younger sister, known for her bravery and survival skills.
  • Skilled in combat, investigation, and adapting to dangerous situations.
  • Strong moral compass and dedication to helping others in crises.
  • Iconic look includes her red jacket and practical attire for survival missions.
  • Has appeared in games, movies, and other media, making her a fan-favorite character.

Games Claire has appeared on


  • Resident Evil 2 (1998) – First appearance as a student searching for her brother in Raccoon City.
  • Resident Evil Code: Veronica (2000) – Plays a central role in uncovering Umbrella Corporation’s secrets.
  • Resident Evil: The Umbrella Chronicles (2007) – Appears in key story retellings.
  • Resident Evil: Revelations 2 (2015) – Main protagonist fighting a new bio-terror outbreak.
  • Resident Evil 2 Remake (2019) – Modernized version of her original 1998 role.
  • Crossovers and spin-offs – Appears in games like Project X Zone and Dead by Daylight.
